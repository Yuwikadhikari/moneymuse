About MoneyMuse

Your smart companion for managing money with ease and confidence.

Who We Are

MoneyMuse is a modern, student-friendly finance tracker designed to help you take control of your income, expenses, and investments. Built with simplicity and clarity in mind, it empowers students, freelancers, and professionals to develop healthy financial habits and achieve their long-term goals.

What We Offer

Track Income & Expenses

Easily record and categorize daily transactions.

Financial Dashboard

Clear snapshot of income, spending, and balance.

Recurring Transactions

Automate regular expenses like rent, fees, or subscriptions.

Budget Planning

Stay on top of limits with alerts and visual insights.

Investment Tracking

Track mutual funds, stocks, and fixed deposits manually.

Custom Categories & Tags

Personalize your finance tracking with meaningful labels.

Our Mission

At MoneyMuse, we believe financial literacy is for everyone. Our goal is to make finance tracking not just easier, but smarter — giving you the tools to plan better, save more, and invest wisely.

"MoneyMuse isn’t just a tracker — it’s your guide to financial freedom."
